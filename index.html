PT. Fan Indonesia Sejahtera is a leader in providing logistics services in Indonesia, established in 2011 with its headquarters in Bekasi. With over a decade of experience, we have developed an extensive infrastructure with 11 offices and storage facilities across the country. Our strength lies in our dedicated team of 265 people and our fleet of 93 vehicles, ensuring timely and efficient deliveries throughout Indonesia,

We take pride in our affiliation with a group of companies including PT. IRBI Pratama Abadi, PT. Cahaya Sentosa Abadi, and PT. Berlian Pratama Indonesia, each specializing in medical device distribution, metal manufacturing, and waste management, respectively. This synergy enhances our capacity to provide comprehensive and sustainable logistics solutions to our customers.

OUR
SERVICE

Cars, Vans, Direct Trucks, and Tractor Trucks:
We offer a wide fleet of vehicles, including cars, vans, direct trucks, and tractor trucks, capable of handling all types of cargo shipments, from small to large deliveries. Our vehicles are equipped with the latest technology to ensure safe and efficient transportation.

Refrigerated Transport:
For shipments requiring temperature control, our refriger-ated service provides an ideal solution. We ensure that products such as food, pharmaceuticals, and temperature sensitive chemicals are transported in optimal conditions to maintain their quality and integrity.

Flatbeds:
Our flatbed service is suitable for transporting large and heavy items that are unsuitable for conventional transport. We specialize in the transport of construction equipment, large machinery, and building materials with safety andreliability.

Hazardous Goods:
We have specialized expertise in transporting hazardous goods. Adhering to all safety regulations, we guarantee the safe delivery of dangerous materials, ensuring that all precautionary measures and safety procedures are strictly followed.

Air Cargo:
Our air cargo service offers a fast solution for international shipments. With a global network and partnerships with leading airlines, we can deliver your goods to almost any location worldwide efficiently and on time.

Hand Carry:
For highly urgent and important delivery needs, our Hand Carry service provides a dedicated courier to personally deliver your items. This is ideal for important documents, product samples, or valuable goods requiring extra handlingandsecurity.

Air Charter:
Our Air Charter service offers specialized aircraft rentals for cargo shipments needing flexible schedules and access to hard-to-reach locations. This solution is perfect for emergencies, large projects, or when speed isatoppriority.
